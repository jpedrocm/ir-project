Networking

The heart of any home or business is its network. Every aspect of daily life is tied so much to the Internet that it doesn’t make sense to have an inferior network. Thankfully, Newegg has everything you need to build a reliable network in your house or office. Whether you’re striving for a smart home, NAS or a surveillance safety system, you’ll find the networking components here.

Correct Components Mean Correct Connections

After you have your high speed Internet installed and data is being processed by the modem, it’s time to get a good router. A model with multiple antennas and frequencies will help your wireless network reach more of your devices with less interference. Just make sure that your computers are all set up to receive the signal. If you have a desktop computer that isn’t already wireless- capable, then a wireless adapter will be necessary.

If you have a large space to cover, then you may want to consider adding a Wi-Fi extender to your network. These devices are designed to receive a wireless signal and then rebroadcast it. This feature is especially handy when trying to deliver wireless coverage between floors.

The Benefits of a Reliable Network

Once you know that data is flowing to and from your devices, it’s time to enjoy the fruits of your labor. Enjoy free international calling with VoIP. Stream your favorite shows over the Internet. Keep your important files in a centralized location with a NAS. The possibilities are nearly endless, but the first step is to check out what networking solutions Newegg offers and choosing what’s right for you. Get started today!
