Computer Systems

For those in the know, Newegg has long been considered the top destination for all your computer needs. Whether you’re looking for a powerful gaming desktop or an agile new portable PC, Newegg is the place to be.

Choosing the right computer

When shopping for a new computer, the first thing you need to do is identify your needs. Do you need a PC that can handle gaming and high-level graphic design or video editing? If that’s the case, then you need to take a close look at the processor and graphics card power in your new system. Brands like Intel, AMD, and NVIDIA are packing more power into PCs than ever, and if it’s been a while since you’ve upgraded, you’ll be amazed at what a new system can do. With a desktop PC from brands like HP, ASUS, or Dell you’ll be getting a lot of hardware bang for your buck. But if portability is more of a concern than raw power, you’ll want to take a look at laptops, notebooks, and 2 in 1 options. These systems can still pack a processing punch, with the added benefit of being light enough to carry to work, class, or on vacation.

Tablets and portable options

At the cutting edge of the computer system world you have tablets, 2 in 1s, and other boundary-pushing portable systems. The latest ASUS Zenbooks include Windows 10 and full keyboards to get your work done on the go, but weigh in at just a few pounds for ultimate portability. The Lenovo Yoga 3 Pro offers an interesting and versatile option capable of four different configurations to maximize the utility of its touchscreen display. Whatever you are looking for in the world of computer systems, Newegg has you covered. Take a look!
